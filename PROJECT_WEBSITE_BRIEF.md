# Modern LMS Platform: Project Brief (Website Content Ready)

## 1) Project Overview
This product is a session-centric Learning and Engagement Platform built for professional training, workshops, and corporate learning programs.

The platform combines structured learning with interactive activities, assessments, collaboration, and analytics in one ecosystem.

At its core, each training program runs through a **Session**. Once users are in a session, they can access all learning and engagement modules such as quizzes, polls, assignments, teams, feedback, and surveys.

## 2) Vision and Purpose
The platform is designed to solve a common problem in corporate learning: content delivery is often disconnected from engagement and measurable outcomes.

The platform unifies:
- learning content delivery
- learner participation
- assessment and scoring
- team collaboration
- admin control and reporting

The goal is to help organizations run high-impact, trackable, and engaging training experiences.

## 3) Who the Platform Serves
- Corporate training teams
- HR and L&D departments
- Training consultants and facilitators
- Organizations running internal certification or capability programs
- Teams conducting employee engagement and feedback cycles

## 4) Platform Roles
### Admin / Session Creator
Admins create and manage sessions, users, learning content, assessments, polls, teams, surveys, and analytics.

### Participant / Learner
Participants join sessions, consume learning materials, complete quizzes and assignments, respond to polls and surveys, join teams, and view their progress.

## 5) Core Product Structure
The product is organized around a **Session-first model**:
1. Admin creates a session
2. Users are invited or assigned (or join via joining code)
3. Session modules become available to participants
4. Learning and engagement data is captured continuously
5. Admin reviews performance and engagement insights

## 6) Major Features

### A) Authentication and Access Management
- Secure login and registration flows
- Email verification and password reset
- Role-based access (admin and participant)
- Protected access to session and module data

### B) Session Management
- Create and update training sessions
- Session joining via unique joining code
- Invite users by email
- Bulk user invite support
- Direct assignment of registered users into sessions
- Session lifecycle and status handling (planned, live, completed, etc.)
- Participant list and participation controls

### C) LMS Learning Module
- Topic-based and level-based learning structure
- Publish/unpublish controls for learning paths
- Mixed content support (video and reading)
- Learning progression rules
- Required completion thresholds
- Level quizzes with pass criteria
- Progress and completion tracking
- Gamified learning experience with progression and leaderboard views

### D) Quiz and Assessment Module
- Session-linked quiz creation and management
- Multiple question formats
- Time limits, scoring rules, and passing criteria
- Participant quiz attempts and submission flows
- Result calculation and pass/fail evaluation
- Quiz leaderboard and comparative performance views
- Per-user detailed result review

### E) Live Polling Module
- Standalone and session-linked polls
- Joining-code based participant access
- Live question control by admin
- Real-time participant response collection
- Support for multiple poll question styles
- Live updates for question state and results
- Participant and response tracking

### F) Assignment Module
- Admin creates assignments at session level
- Configurable deadlines and late-submission rules
- File-type and file-size controls
- Multi-file submissions
- Submission replacement/version handling
- Submission status visibility (submitted, late, missing)
- Downloadable submitted files
- Assignment timeline/audit events

### G) Team Management and Points
- Team creation within sessions
- Manual and structured participant assignment to teams
- Team member role control (for example, leader/member)
- Team capacity and configuration management
- Team and individual point awarding
- Team leaderboard and performance ranking

### H) Content Management
- Upload and manage session learning resources
- Metadata-backed content library per session
- Controlled access based on session participation
- Download/access support for participants

### I) Feedback Module
- Session-specific feedback form creation
- Multi-question forms
- Anonymous or identified feedback modes
- Smiley-scale and text feedback collection
- Response aggregation for admin analysis
- Feedback form activation/deactivation controls

### J) Organization and Survey Module
- Organization setup inside session context
- Department and team hierarchy mapping
- User assignment to organization units
- Survey creation with flexible question types
- Assignment to departments, teams, or individuals
- Session-based and broader organizational survey use cases
- Survey response analytics and reporting

### K) Dashboard and Analytics
- Admin dashboard with platform and session summaries
- Progress, participation, and performance indicators
- Leaderboards for quizzes/teams/LMS progress
- Reporting-oriented visibility for decision making

### L) Real-time Experience
- Real-time interactions for live poll workflows
- Live participant synchronization for active questions and updates
- Fast engagement during facilitator-led sessions

- Combines content, interaction, and measurement in one flow
- Designed for professional and organizational training scenarios
